The Musician's 101
OVER 8 HOURS OF CONTENT Learn To:
Write Record Make Instrumentals Create Graphics Edit Music Videos Promote Your Music
ALL 100% FREE!
This collection of videos was designed to be an educational guide for anyone with an interest in creating music. While there is a strong emphasis on hip hop, the Musician's 101 outlines how to record vocals, produce your own instrumental music, create graphics, edit your own videos, promote your content, and everything else necessary for an upcoming artist to begin creating any type of music of music they choose to. All in all it is over 8 hours of content. This course was designed for complete beginners, so you do not need any previous experience. My goal is not to make you an expert on any individual topic, but to give you an introduction to all of these fields and give you guidance on where to turn to continue mastering that subject. If you've already been making music for a while, some of this information may include things you already know, but I hope that there are at least a few new and ideas that are new to you.
