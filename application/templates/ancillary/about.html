About Me

Hi! My name is Alan. I was born in St. Petersburg, Florida with Bilaterial Microtia and Atresia. Because of this, I am mostly deaf. Around the age of 12, I began writing songs and experimenting with music production, as well as utilizing Adobe Photoshop to create basic graphic designs.

After I turning 21, I moved to Maryland where I performed Stand Up Comedy at a number of open mics and competitions. It was in Maryland that I met my wife, Brooke, who has been the best part of my life for over 8 years.

Together, we moved to Mobile, Alabama with our adorable calico, Chloe. I made the decision to return to college to complete my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I've accumulated a number of hobbies and interests over the years, and hope to accumulate more as I get older. I have a strong passion for learning as much as I can, and try to constantly develop new projects as I go.
